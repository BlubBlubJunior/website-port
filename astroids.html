Dating Sim

View Github
What's it about
I worked with two a three artists on a dating sim game. In the game, you follow a story and play some mini-games.
What have I learned
This was my first experience creating a game with artists, and it didn't go smoothly right from the beginning. However, it taught me the importance of time management and putting in hard work, even if it meant staying up all night to complete the project. I also gained valuable insights into what makes a good game loop and what can make one ineffective.
What could I improve
If I spend 5 to 10 more hours on this project, I'm gonna change how the game loops work. I'll add a slider for affection, so dialogues only show up when you've got enough affection. Also, I'll fix the music.
